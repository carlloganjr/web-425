Mozart

Austrian composer and performer Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart belonged to the Classical era. From an early age, he displayed extraordinary talent. He began composing at the age of five and performed for European royalty while being already proficient on the keyboard and violin. At age 17, he was hired as a court musician in Salzburg. However, he soon became restless and moved around in pursuit of a better job while producing great music. Many of his most well-known symphonies, concertos, operas, and pieces of the Requiem, which was largely incomplete at the time of his untimely death, were written during his final years.
